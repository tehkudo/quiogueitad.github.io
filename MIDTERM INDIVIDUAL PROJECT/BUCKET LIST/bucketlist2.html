My Bucket List


        A bucket list's main goal is to live a life full with goals and dreams. Making a bucket list enables us to reflect on our beliefs and ambitions, as well as pinpoint significant milestones and events that we wish to encounter during our lives. Making a bucket list is a fun method to figure out which activities and experiences you believe would offer you happiness and fulfillment in life. This list may serve as a road map for constructing a life you enjoy as long as you don't get too caught up in feeling like you have to complete everything on it. Here are some of items that I want to accomplish in my bucket list:


  1. Journey to the Grand Canyon
  2. Experience the Northern Lights
  3. Be on a TV Show
  4. Become fluent in a new language
  5. Take a hot air balloon ride
  6. Travel in an Airplane
  7. Own my own car
  8. Own my own property
  9. Go camping with friends or family
  10. Attend a Concert
  11. Attend a Comic-Con
  12. Get a Job
  13. Take a Road Trip
  14. Throw Someone a Surprise Party
  15. Go to an Amusement Park
