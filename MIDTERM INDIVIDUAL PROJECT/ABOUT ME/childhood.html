Revisiting the Past

Childhood Years

     My Childhood Years was pretty fun. Especially when I started making friends.1 I still remember my first day of school, of course like most of the first time students, I cried because we were so young and confused.2 But I got used to it and had fun in my classroom, playing with my classmates and teacher.3 I also had many toys back then that I still cherish up 'til now.4 I also still have contact on some of my childhood friends and classmates and we still hang out.5 I forgot the school I went to back then, but it's a barangay school.6 Back in my kindergarten days, I still remember how much fun I had then. Our section even became a small choir back then. In my elementary, I transfered to Cainta Catholic College (CCC) one of the local schools here in Cainta6. It was fun because we got to go on a field trip and it was really awesome got to meet a lot of new classmates and made friends with some of them.7 I had tons of great memories there and I really miss my old school.8 Every break time me and my classmates hangout around our campus.9 If we're not hanging around, we play taya-tayaan or bente uno or whatever game we think.10 Sometimes we just play together on our phones with games.11 Sometimes, some of my classmates bring guitars so sometimes we just jam along.12 I still remember waking up in the morning watching cartoons while I eats breakfast.13 Then, when I was waiting for my mom to pick me up to go home, I play with other kids to pass time.14 I also still remember our first dog, we name her 'chocolate', she was a very good dog.15 After we got our first dog we were given a second one and we named him 'brownie', then we got a puppy and we named him 'soxy'.16 When there was no school, I used to hangout and play with my neighbors all day.17 We play a lot of games, but the most game we played was hide and seek when its evening.18 And when its christmas time, we often go caroling and spend our money to celebrate.19 So, all I can say is, my childhood was very good and very fun.20
